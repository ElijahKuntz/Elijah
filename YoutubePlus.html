YouTube+

What if you could segment your subscriptions based on category, creators, or however you'd like? Well, now you can. Introducing YouTube+, an addon to YouTube creating subscription lists that allows you create them just like video lists.

Motivation

How might we?

How might we make a more competent form of YouTube premium called YouTube+ that is aimed less at additional premiums(ie. music/tv) and more at improved user experience. This could include but not dislike visibility, ad control or removal, or sorting options. 

Research Summary

We found through observations, surveys, and a competitive product survey that a majority of people believe that YouTube could be improved, and that they believe implementing things such as subscription lists would increase the benefits of YouTube premium

Key Takeaways

- YouTube's competitors have many features that YouTube is lacking and could benefit from adding
- YouTube has been introducing too many advertisements
- Many of YouTube's recent changes such as removing the dislike button are harming the platform
